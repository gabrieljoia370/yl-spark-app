YL Spark

Plan less. Play more.

A small set of tools to help teachers of 3–12 year olds draft lessons, adapt activities and build vocab sets in seconds. Built on Cambridge YL, British Council and Colorín Colorado — but it sounds like a teacher, not a textbook.

Spark a full lesson

Tell me about your class — age, level, topic, time. I'll draft a complete lesson with warmer, presentation, controlled and freer practice, and a cooler. Plus teacher language, materials, and a quick check.

Usually ready in 10–20 seconds.

Adapt any activity

Paste an activity from your coursebook (or memory). I'll reshape it for your age and level — simpler, more physical, more visual, or scaffolded for mixed ability.

Usually ready in 10–15 seconds.

Build a vocab set

Pick a topic. Get printable flashcards with kid-friendly example sentences, a chant or song hook, and three classroom games you can use right away.

Cards + chant + 3 mini-games.
